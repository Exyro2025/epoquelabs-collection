The Époquelle™ Daily Ritual

A simple, clinically mapped sequence you can live with every day. The same four pillars— cleanse, focus, regenerate, seal—repeated morning and night, with intensity adjusted by your clinician.

Morning · Éclat & Protection

Light, hydrating, SPF-ready

Keep it light, hydrating, and protective while preparing the skin for SPF and makeup.

  • Step 1 · Cleanse — No. 13
    Massage onto dry skin until it melts to oil, then emulsify with water into a milk. Rinse gently.
  • Step 2 · Eye Focus — No. 6
    Press a rice-grain amount around the orbital bone, avoiding direct contact with the eye.
  • Step 3 · Regenerate — No. 9
    Apply 1–2 pumps to face, neck and décolleté for peptide-driven firmness and tone.
  • Step 4 · Seal — Scellé™
    Finish with a whisper-thin layer to lock in actives and support barrier comfort.
  • Final · SPF
    Follow with broad-spectrum mineral or hybrid SPF as directed by your clinician.

Evening · Repair & Renewal

Deeper repair, slower ritual

Evening is where deeper repair happens. The same pillars, with slower application and longer contact time.

  • Step 1 · Cleanse — No. 13
    Use as a first and second cleanse to dissolve SPF, makeup, and pollutants.
  • Step 2 · Eye Focus — No. 6
    Press gently into expression lines and areas of fatigue around the eyes.
  • Step 3 · Regenerate — No. 9
    Apply 1–2 pumps, taking time to massage along jawline, cheekbones, and neck.
  • Step 4 · Seal — Scellé™
    Layer last as an overnight finishing veil to support barrier repair and prolong in-clinic results.

Clinician Adjustments: Your provider may layer professional peels, boosters, or devices around this core ritual according to your protocol.
