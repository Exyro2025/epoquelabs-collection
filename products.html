The Époquelle™ System

A Four-Phase Ritual of Refinement

The Époquelle™ ritual moves in four quiet phases: cleanse, illuminate, hydrate, and seal. The structure can be adjusted in-clinic or at home—yet the experience on the skin remains the same.

Phase I · Cleanse

Gelée Purifiante No. 1

A clarifying gel cleanser infused with gentle fruit acids to refine the surface, brighten tone, and prepare the skin for active treatment. Leaves a clean, quiet, glass-like finish.

Designed as the first step every morning and evening. Suitable for most skin types, including refinement-focused and post-procedure when directed by your clinician.

Phase II · Illuminate

Sérum Lumière C15 No. 7

A daily vitamin C15 serum that visibly improves clarity, luminosity, and environmental resilience. Designed for stability and potency, with an elegant slip that layers quietly under the next step.

Used in the morning to brighten and support visible firmness over time.

Join Waitlist · Sérum Lumière C15
Phase III · Hydrate

Floraderm Hydratique No. 9

A multi-weight hyaluronic serum delivering deep hydration and surface smoothness. Strengthens the moisture barrier while enhancing suppleness and clarity.

Applied after vitamin C or other treatment serums to cushion and hydrate without heaviness.

Join Waitlist · Hydratique No. 9
Phase IV · Seal

Crème Éclat Radiance No. 13

A barrier-supportive radiance crème that seals hydration, refines texture, and enhances natural luminosity. Leaves a soft, refined, illuminated finish.

The final step before SPF in the morning, or over treatment serums at night.

Orbital Focus

Contour Diffusion Eye Crème No. 6

A smoothing eye crème that diffuses the look of fine lines, fatigue, and shadow. Restores softness and luminosity to the delicate orbital area.

Press gently around the orbital bone morning and night, after serum and before crème.

Introduced gradually in the evening ritual at a cadence set by your clinician or skin specialist.

Used one to two evenings per week after cleansing, followed by hydrating serum and crème.

Everyday Use

The Époquelle™ Daily Ritual

A mapped sequence you can live with every day. The same four pillars—cleanse, illuminate, hydrate, seal—repeated morning and night, with intensity adjusted by your clinician. Retinol and the peptide mask are reserved for quiet, weekly refinement.

Morning · Éclat & Protection

Core Day Ritual

  • Step 1 · Cleanse
    Gelée Purifiante No. 1
  • Step 2 · Illuminate
    Sérum Lumière C15 No. 7
  • Step 3 · Hydrate
    Floraderm Hydratique No. 9
  • Step 4 · Seal
    Crème Éclat Radiance No. 13
  • Step 5 · Eye Focus
    Contour Diffusion Eye Crème No. 6
  • Final · SPF
    Broad-spectrum mineral or hybrid SPF as directed by your clinician.
Evening · Repair & Renewal

Core Night Ritual

  • Step 1 · Cleanse
    Gelée Purifiante No. 1 (single or double cleanse as needed).
  • Step 2 · Hydrate or Treat
    Floraderm Hydratique No. 9, or on scheduled nights, Crème Rétinol Nuit No. 12 as directed.
  • Step 3 · Seal
    Crème Éclat Radiance No. 13
  • Step 4 · Eye Focus
    Contour Diffusion Eye Crème No. 6
  • Weekly · Masque
    Masque Radiance Peptidique No. 14 one to two evenings per week after cleansing, before crème.

Clinician Adjustments: your provider may layer professional peels, boosters, or devices around this core ritual according to your protocol.
