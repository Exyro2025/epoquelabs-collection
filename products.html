A Four-Phase Ritual for Regeneration

Époquelle™ is the home ritual expression of Époque Labs—clinical formulas translated into a simple, repeatable sequence: cleanse, focus, regenerate, seal. Each phase is designed to stand alone yet read as one continuous story on the skin.

The Époquelle™ Daily Ritual

A mapped sequence you can live with every day. The same four pillars—cleanse, focus, regenerate, seal—repeated morning and night, with intensity adjusted by your clinician.

Morning · Éclat & Protection

Light, hydrating, SPF-ready

Keep it light and protective while preparing the skin for SPF and the day’s exposure.

  • Step 1 · Cleanse
    Massage the transforming cream onto dry skin until it melts to oil, then emulsify with water into a milk. Rinse gently.
  • Step 2 · Focus
    Press a rice-grain amount of eye concentrate around the orbital bone, avoiding direct contact with the eye.
  • Step 3 · Regenerate
    Apply 1–2 pumps of peptide serum to face, neck, and décolleté.
  • Step 4 · Seal
    Finish with a whisper-thin layer of barrier veil to lock in actives.
  • Final · SPF
    Follow with broad-spectrum mineral or hybrid SPF as directed by your clinician.

Evening · Repair & Renewal

Deeper repair, slower ritual

Evening is where deeper repair happens. The same pillars, with slower application and longer contact time.

  • Step 1 · Cleanse
    Use the cream as first and second cleanse to dissolve SPF, pollution, and the day.
  • Step 2 · Focus
    Press gently into expression lines and areas of fatigue around the eyes.
  • Step 3 · Regenerate
    Apply 1–2 pumps, taking time to massage along jawline, cheekbones, and neck.
  • Step 4 · Seal
    Layer the finishing veil last to support barrier repair and prolong in-clinic results.

Clinician Adjustments: Your provider may layer professional peels, boosters, or devices around this core ritual according to your protocol.
