Hosts

Elise

Clinical Ritual · Co-Host

Elise brings a calm, clinical lens to every episode—grounded in results-driven skincare and long-term skin health. She speaks the language of ingredients, protocols, and what actually happens between the treatment room and your bathroom sink.

André

Perspective & Practice · Co-Host

André balances the conversation with lived experience, curious questions, and a focus on how real people navigate skin decisions. He asks what you would ask—and occasionally what you didn’t know you needed to hear.
