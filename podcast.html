Hosts

Elise

Clinical Ritual · Co-Host

Elise Marais is the person you wish appeared in every consultation—steady, clear, and quietly exacting. She translates clinical language into choices you can actually live with: what deserves investment, what can be skipped, and how to build a routine that respects both your barrier and your real life. Her background spans treatment rooms, ingredient research, and the slow, disciplined art of skin repair. On the mic, she is measured, reassuring, and gently protective of both your skin and your budget—bringing the precision of a clinician with the warmth of someone who has seen every kind of barrier story.

André

Perspective & Practice · Co-Host

André Solane is the anchor for the listener—the voice that asks the things you were thinking but weren’t sure you were supposed to say out loud. He comes from the lived side of skincare: trial-and-error routines, treatment regrets, product overload, and the quiet exhaustion of trying to look rested in an overextended world. His role is to keep the conversations human and grounded in how it actually feels to be in your skin—not just what looks good on a treatment menu. He brings honesty, curiosity, and a sense of relief to every episode, reminding you that good skincare is as much about real life as it is about results.
