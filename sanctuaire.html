What Sanctuaire Is

Époque Sanctuaire™ is the environment to our treatment — a discreet, curated refuge for post-procedure recovery. Each suite pairs quiet-luxury interiors with spa-grade comforts: blackout rest, reclining recovery seating, in-suite tea rituals, and Époque’s signature soundscapes.

Quiet-Luxury Suites

Optional partners for IV hydration, private duty nursing, and discreet transport — on your schedule.
