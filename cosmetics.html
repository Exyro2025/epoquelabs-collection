Époque Maison des Lèvres

The lip line that begins where skincare ends.

Built around one idea: when the skin is cared for, color should feel like punctuation, not performance. Époque Maison des Lèvres pairs clinical care with quietly expressive shades, so lips feel protected, hydrated, and distinctly Époque—at every age.
