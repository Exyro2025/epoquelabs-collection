ÉPOQUE LABS · ÉPOQUE MAISON DES LÈVRES™

The mouth, written in quiet light.

Époque Maison des Lèvres™ is a small lip house inside Époque Labs—treatment, glass-light gloss, and liquid velvet color composed like sentences across the mouth. Every formula is built to sit beside Époquelle™ skincare and Skincare Without Surgery™ conversations, so the face feels considered as a whole, not divided into parts.

Shades are intentionally few, deliberately edited, and designed to feel like structure rather than costume: soft neutrals, deep wine, and a clear architecture of shine.

LES COULEURS D’ÉPOQUE™ · HOUSE STATEMENT

A lip line that begins where skincare ends.

When the skin is cared for, color should feel like punctuation, not performance. Époque Maison des Lèvres™ pairs clinical-feeling care with quietly expressive shades, so lips feel protected, hydrated, and distinctly Époque—at every age.

Les Couleurs d’Époque™ — crafted in quiet elegance, worn in undeniable presence.

PILLAR I · THE LIP RITUAL

Care pieces for daily ritual.

The Maison line begins with care. These formulas behave like skincare—smoothing, softening, and shielding the lip surface so every shade lays down more beautifully and wears longer. Morning and night, alone or under color.

Voile Vitamine E™

Clear vitamin E lipstick · Universal veil

A clear veil of restorative luxury. Enriched with vitamin E, this treatment stick melts on contact, wrapping the mouth in weightless hydration and a soft, glass-like sheen. The first step in every Époque lip ritual.

  • Antioxidant-rich
  • Wears under any shade
  • Day or night

Baume de Minuit™

Night balm · Soft-matte cushion

A cushiony nighttime balm that softens as you sleep. Lips wake smoother, more supple, and ready for color with less tug and fewer lines.

  • Overnight repair
  • Feather-soft texture
  • No heavy residue

Éclisse Scrub™

Micro-fine sugar exfoliator

A neutral-base sugar polish with a micro-fine grain. Smooths away roughness without a gritty afterfeel, leaving the lip line refined and ready for precise application.

  • Pre-color prep
  • No grainy film
  • Softens texture

L’Étude Lip Serum™

Lightweight serum veil

A featherweight, clinical-feeling treatment for days when lips need more. Wears alone for a bare, cared-for look or under color for extra comfort and a soft, diffused finish.

  • Featherweight slip
  • Can be worn alone
  • Layer under velvet

Essence de Lèvres™

Sheer gloss-treatment veil

Hydration, light, and a whisper of fullness. This gloss-treatment hybrid behaves like care and looks like glass, bringing soft plumpness without sting or tingling—only elegance.

  • Glass-light shine
  • Soft fullness (no burn)
  • Wears over any shade

All treatment pieces are universal and sheer, chosen so any skin tone can step into the line beginning with care.

PILLARS II & III · COLOR WARDROBE

A small, edited wardrobe of gloss and velvet.

Color in Époque Maison des Lèvres™ is quiet by design. Two glass-light glosses, one crystal clear, and three liquid velvet shades with a soft-focus, fabric-like finish. Worn alone, layered, or diffused with fingertip for a blurred Paris-at-dusk lip.

Glasslight Gloss Line “Light, lacquer, and the subtle architecture of shine.”

Brillantine d’Époque™

Warm nude gloss · Soft gold micro-shimmer

Soft peach-beige with quiet gold reflection. A warm nude that reads as polished skin rather than glitter—perfect alone or as a finishing veil over Matin de Voile™.

  • Everyday nude
  • Subtle dimension
  • Suited to all tones

Voix de Satin™

Neutral rose gloss · Satin sheen

A neutral rose-beige with satin shine—elegant, grown, and calm on the mouth. Lends quiet color to bare lips or softens the edge of deeper shades like Noir Grenat™.

  • Universal rose
  • Office to evening
  • Layer-friendly

Nuage Clair™

Crystal clear gloss · Glass-light finish

Pure clarity with Époque restraint. Wears alone for a clean, light-catching lip or over any Maison shade without altering its undertone.

  • Glass effect
  • Non-sticky
  • Works over everything

Liquid Velvet Line “Soft-matte veils for women who speak without raising their voice.”

Matin de Voile™

Rose nude liquid velvet

Soft-matte with a morning-light hush. A muted rose-nude that feels like silk and looks like breathless quiet—the everyday lip, perfected.

  • Day-to-night neutral
  • Soft-focus finish
  • Beautiful under gloss

Silence Matiné™

Soft neutral liquid velvet

Beige-mink neutrality. The “no-makeup makeup” matte that frames the face without announcing itself. Worn alone or as a base, it leaves only structure and intention.

  • Understated chic
  • Medium-depth neutral
  • Pairs with any gloss

Noir Grenat™

Deep wine liquid velvet

Garnet depth, midnight certainty. A deep wine shade shaped for evenings, impact, and the unspoken pull of quiet seduction. Bold without volume, powerful without noise.

  • Statement shade
  • Soft-matte, not flat
  • Dial down with Voix de Satin™

The Maison des Lèvres palette was edited to be wearable across undertones: fair, medium, olive, deep, and rich. Quiet, not anonymous.
