Application ritual

Three steps. One composed lip.

Clients can wear each formula alone, but the suite was designed to layer. The result is a lip that looks finished yet unforced—suitable for clinic, boardroom, and after-dark without a full rework.

  • Step 01 · Treat Apply a thin layer of Lip Treatment Ensemble and allow it to settle for 3–5 minutes. Tissue-blot before colour for a smoother, more even surface.
  • Step 02 · Define Sketch the natural lip line with a soft-toned pencil, focusing on corners and Cupid’s bow. The line should look like shadow, not liner.
  • Step 03 · Veil Sweep Light Veil Lipstick from centre outward, then tap Liquid Veil Lipgloss onto the centre of the lower lip for a controlled, luminous highlight.
