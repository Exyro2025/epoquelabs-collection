Clinical-Grade Positioning

What we mean by “clinical-grade.”

“Clinical-grade” is more than a phrase. For Époque Labs, it means working with laboratories experienced in professional treatment back bars, using actives at purposeful levels—not just label mentions—and prioritizing tolerance and long-term use over single-use drama. Every formula on this page is designed to sit comfortably beside your dermatologist’s and aesthetician’s protocols.
