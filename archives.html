Chapter I – The Forgotten Apothecary

Émile d’Aragon was a quiet theorist of the skin — an apothecary working in the shadows of Provence. He believed that skin was more than tissue; it was a memory, a rhythm, a response. From 1919 to 1929, he compiled over 200 regenerative treatments inside a sealed leather-bound ledger, later known as the Compendium d’Aragon.

Chapter II – A Discovery in Dust

In the spring of 2025, Époque Labs acquired a private estate archive in Aix-en-Provence. Inside: botanical sketches, vintage suspensions, and Émile’s handwritten epidermal theory — decades ahead of modern regenerative science. These pages became the foundation of Époque’s rebirth.

Chapter III – La Méthode Émile™

His treatment system, now called La Méthode Émile™, follows a precise 4-phase ritual: Purify. Signal. Revive. Shield. Every Époque formula is built on this timeless choreography — blending clinical-grade ingredients with poetic application.

Chapter IV – The Compendium Lives

For the first time in history, the Compendium d’Aragon has been restored, annotated, and protected. Époque Labs now brings his vision to the world — through formulas, rituals, and intellectual property unlike any other.
